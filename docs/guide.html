How much does this cost?

The discovery and filtering are free. The AI scoring step uses the Claude API, which charges based on usage. A typical run scans 2,000+ jobs, filters down to a few hundred, and costs a few dollars to score. You'll need an API key from console.anthropic.com — Claude Code will help you set it up.

🎯

Scores jobs on fit, not keywords

Claude reads the full job description and compares it to your career story — your skills, your experience, where you're headed. A keyword matcher would miss context. Claude gets it.

🤝

Knows who you know

If you upload your LinkedIn connections, the tool checks whether you know someone at each company. Jobs where you have a warm intro get boosted — because that actually matters.

💰

Reads between the salary lines

Even when a job doesn't list compensation, Claude estimates whether it's likely above or below your target based on the company, role level, and market context.

📝

Tells you why

Every score comes with reasoning — what matches, what doesn't, and what you'd need to address in your application. It's a cheat sheet for your cover letter.

What to Do Next

The report is your starting point. Here's how to get the most out of it.

🎯

Chase the 90+ scores

Scores above 90 are rare. When one shows up, it's genuinely worth your attention. Don't let it sit.

🤝

Reach out to connections first

When you have a connection at a company, message them before applying. A warm intro is worth 10 cold applications.

📝

Use the reasoning in your cover letter

Claude tells you exactly what overlaps and what gaps exist. Lead with the overlaps. Address the gaps proactively.

🔄

Keep tuning your profile

If roles you like are scoring low, your profile might need adjusting. Just tell Claude Code what to change.

Bonus: LinkedIn Data

Two optional upgrades that make the tool smarter.

👥

Your connections

Export your LinkedIn connections (Settings → Data Privacy → Get a copy of your data) and tell Claude Code where the file is. The tool will flag every job where you know someone at the company.

🏢

Companies you follow

If your LinkedIn export includes Saved Jobs or Company Follows, tell Claude Code to expand your monitoring list. It'll auto-detect which career pages it can poll and add them.

Your Data Stays Yours

Your profile, API keys, LinkedIn connections, and search results never leave your machine. Everything personal is gitignored — it's just for you.
