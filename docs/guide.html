What to Do Next

The report is your starting point. Here's how to act on it.

🎯

Chase the 90+ scores

They're rare. When one shows up, it's genuinely worth your attention. Don't let it sit — these are the roles that actually fit your trajectory.

🤝

Reach out to connections first

When the tool flags someone you know at a company, message them before applying. A warm intro is worth 10 cold applications.

📝

Use the reasoning in your cover letter

Claude tells you exactly what overlaps and what gaps exist. Lead with the overlaps. Address the gaps proactively. You have a cheat sheet — use it.

🔄

Keep tuning

If roles you like are scoring low, your profile needs adjusting. Just tell Claude Code what to change. The next search will be sharper.

Make It Smarter

Your LinkedIn data is the highest-leverage upgrade. Add it anytime — no need to set it up during initial setup.

👥

Network matching

Export your connections and the tool flags every job where you know someone at the company. A warm intro changes your odds — the scoring engine knows it and boosts those matches.

🏢

Company monitoring

Your Saved Jobs and Company Follows tell the tool which companies you care about. It auto-detects their career pages and monitors them, so you never miss a posting.

How to get your LinkedIn data

LinkedIn → Settings → Data Privacy → Get a copy of your data. Select "Connections" at minimum. Also select "Jobs" and "Company Follows" for the full benefit. LinkedIn emails you a ZIP within ~24 hours. Unzip it into the project folder and tell Claude Code "I added my LinkedIn data."

Your Data Stays Yours

Your profile, API keys, LinkedIn connections, and search results never leave your machine. Everything personal is gitignored — it's just for you.
